spinning up the multiverse…

Operators are honest parameter transforms on the bivariate-Poisson goal rates — the quantum names describe exactly what they do to the odds (shown before→after). The Daily Challenge is one shared, frozen timeline ranked on the global board; Free Play re-rolls the field every run. λ (lambda) is the entanglement independent models miss.
